COTTON WASTE LICKERIN

NEXT

Specification

Lickerin is also a by-product obtained during yarn manufacturing. Being a carding waste product, it is low in cost and fiber rich. It is a valuable source for OE spinning of low count yarns.

Details

  • BLEND: 100% cotton Themes
  • LENGTH: 23-25 m.m.
  • MIC: 30-35%
  • COLOR: Natural

Features

  • It is fiber rich
  • Best used in 7’s to 21’s counts yarns in open end spinning

Application

  • Open end spinning
  • Towel Yarn
  • Denim Yarn
