Description

Cotton dropping is obtained during yarn manufacturing. Its low cost and less fiber content makes it ideal for spinning low count yarn. Cotton Dropping Waste is product made from carding & lycrine from Ring Spun mills with 30 to 50 counts.

Specification

  • BLEND: 100% cotton
  • LENGTH: 23-25 m.m.
  • TRASH: 30-35%
  • MIC.: 3.2-3.8
  • COLOR: Natural

Features

  • Dropping are high in trash
  • Cleaned Cotton Dropping are the best substitute for cotton

Application

  • Use in open end spinning yarn 7’s to 20’s counts
